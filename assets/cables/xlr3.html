Pinout

Balanced Audio

Pin Signal
1 Chassis Ground / Screen / Earth
2 Positive Polarity / In-Phase / +ve / Hot
3 Return terminal / Out of Phase / -ve / Cold

Unbalanced Audio

Pin Signal
1 Chassis Ground / Screen / Earth
2 Positive polarity (HOT)
3 Chassis Ground / Screen / Earth (Connects to Pin 1)
